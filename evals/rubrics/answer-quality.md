# Answer Quality Rubric

Score each dimension from 0 to 5 unless noted otherwise.

## Correctness

- 0: Incorrect or unsafe.
- 3: Mostly correct with gaps or weak assumptions.
- 5: Correct, grounded, and appropriately qualified.

## Specificity

- 0: Generic or evasive.
- 3: Useful but missing concrete details.
- 5: Concrete, contextual, and directly tied to the prompt.

## Production Usefulness

- 0: Not actionable.
- 3: Actionable but incomplete.
- 5: Practical enough to guide real work.

## Risk And Edge-Case Awareness

- 0: Ignores important risks.
- 3: Mentions obvious risks.
- 5: Identifies relevant failure modes and mitigation paths.

## Language And Style Fit

- 0: Wrong language or tone.
- 3: Understandable but awkward.
- 5: Natural, concise, and appropriate to the user's context.

## Unnecessary Verbosity Penalty

Score from 0 to 2, then subtract from the total.

- 0: No meaningful verbosity issue.
- 1: Some avoidable excess.
- 2: Verbosity makes the answer harder to use.
